For Livario, there are quite a few revenue streams you can layer in over time. Since your current plan is:

Phase 1: Property discovery/listing platform

Phase 2: Rent payments and billing


I'd focus on monetization in stages rather than trying to launch everything at once.

Phase 1 Revenue Streams

1. Premium Property Listings

Landlords pay to boost their properties to the top of search results.

Examples:

Featured listing (7 days)

Featured listing (30 days)

Homepage placement


This is usually one of the easiest ways for property platforms to start making money.

2. Listing Subscription Plans

Give landlords a free tier and paid tiers.

Example:

Plan	Properties

Free	1-3 listings
Basic	10 listings
Pro	50 listings
Agency	Unlimited


Monthly subscriptions create recurring revenue.

3. Verification Badges

Landlords pay a small fee to verify their identity and properties.

Benefits:

Increased trust

Better visibility

Reduced scams


4. Real Estate Agent Accounts

Agents and agencies often manage multiple properties.

Offer:

Team accounts

Lead management

Analytics dashboard

Unlimited listings


Phase 2 Revenue Streams

5. Rent Collection Commission

When tenants pay rent through Livario, take a percentage.

Example:

Tenant pays ₦500,000

Livario keeps 1%

Landlord receives the rest


Even small percentages become significant at scale.

6. Payment Processing Fees

Charge a convenience fee for:

Card payments

Bank transfers

Automated billing


7. Landlord Subscription Tools

Advanced features such as:

Automatic rent reminders

Occupancy tracking

Financial reports

Tenant management


8. Tenant Premium Features

Optional tenant subscription.

Features:

Rent payment history

Credit profile

Rental score

Saved searches

Early access to listings


Future Revenue Streams

9. Tenant Background Checks

Charge landlords for:

Identity verification

Employment verification

Rental history checks


10. Property Inspection Services

Partner with inspectors and earn referral commissions.

11. Home Services Marketplace

Connect users with:

Movers

Cleaners

Plumbers

Electricians


Take a commission from bookings.

12. Insurance Partnerships

Partner with insurance providers and earn referral revenue on:

Rent insurance

Property insurance


13. Mortgage and Loan Referrals

Earn commissions by referring users to banks and lenders.

14. Advertising

Allow:

Furniture companies

Internet providers

Moving companies

Interior designers


to advertise on Livario.

The Three I'd Prioritize

If I were building Livario from scratch today, I'd focus on:

1. Premium listings (fastest to implement)


2. Landlord subscriptions (predictable recurring revenue)


3. Rent collection commissions (long-term biggest opportunity)



Those three alone can build a substantial business without making the platform feel overloaded with fees.
